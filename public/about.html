What is this?

This website contains samples of text generated from GPT-2, fine-tuned on text from Literotica.

What is 'Conditioned'?

'Conditioned' samples are generated conditioning on the first few words of chapters from select books from Project Gutenberg, as well as some short priming text.

What is 'Generated'?

'Generated' samples are generated without conditioning on any starting text.
