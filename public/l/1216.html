Les Misérables by Victor Hugo

CHAPTER VI—FUTURE PROGRESS

The excavation of the sewer of Paris has been no slight task. The last ten centuries have toiled at it without being able to bring it to a termination, any other time. Yet, here today is a Day of Repose, which means the day of my resurrection comes. Let's hope this week is my only week at peace. For this I hold and to hold, let's give thanks for the day all will be finished.
