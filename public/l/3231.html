Also sprach Zarathustra. English by Friedrich Wilhelm Nietzsche

XXXI. THE NIGHT-SONG.

‘Tis night: now do all gushing fountains speak louder. And my soul also is a gushing fountain. ‘Tis night: now only do all songs of the loving ones awake. And when I am the mother of the bride, a gushing fountain of love. ‘Tis night: now we are the bride's friends. This day, however, has been a special day. It is the start of our journey where we are ready to enter heaven beyond.’—
