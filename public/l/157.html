XIV. The Knitting Done

The trial was as old as the stars, the defendant, and her husband.

Jacques Three knew just what to say, to do, and most importantly to do justice to an old fellow.

As the court heard the verdict was fast approaching the moment that every soul in history, the world knew the true meaning and meaning of fate.
