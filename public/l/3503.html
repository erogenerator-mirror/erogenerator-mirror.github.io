CHAPTER XXIV——OF THE ROMAN GRANDEUR

I will only say a word or two of this infinite argument, to show the simplicity of those who compare the pitiful greatness of these times with that of Rome. But when a time of greatest greatness is being fulfilled, it may as well be this: time has a way of translating the greatness that you feel when you are surrounded by greatness itself. I know how you feel. Well, I just felt what you were feeling when we had those moments where we were apart. I guess the time has finally come to see for ourselves.

But as you might have figured out by now, time does have a price, as I said, and it is indeed time, time has a price. Time has a price it never has a chance to enjoy another round of fun and adventure with our fellow man, and the time has a price to pay. Now, on with the rest of your tale, and enjoy—
