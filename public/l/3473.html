CHAPTER L——OF DEMOCRITUS AND HERACLITUS

The judgment is an utensil proper for all subjects, and will have an oar in everything: which is the reason, that in these Essays I take hold of all occasions and do so with the proper regard of what happens in them.

I want to ask you to please, what with all those "forbidden" words and sentences, the right question to ask.
