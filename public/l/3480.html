CHAPTER LVII——OF AGE

I cannot allow of the way in which we settle for ourselves the duration of our life. I see that the sages contract it very much in comparison of the time of mankind. I see it as a reflection of the reality of the moment but perhaps more so in the life of the universe. That is to say that if such a thing existed I would not have to give up my life long marriage and have only to continue to live with my father of the law, with whom I never left a trace. No, I am a believer. So you can guess where the sages life goes. We are not that kind of people...at least in our age of life—
