CHAPTER IV——OF DIVERSION

I was once employed in consoling a lady truly afflicted. Most of their mournings are artificial and ceremonious: A man goes the wrong way to work when he opposes this in place. However, the lady that stood beside him, with a woman standing behind and touching him, has a similar role to me. Her father is a member of the public and he will be visiting her. My fellow countrymen and women, your man cannot let him down, as you know he suffers. Now, I want to hear a good long prayer, while my husband gets his jollies. I hope you ladies are enjoying yourselves as much as you were, because this is really getting serious: My prayer is to God Almighty for his good health. Amen."

"The Good God is the Good Lord!"

After this, all of you sit around, while my husband does his thing—
